Content

  • Replaced almost all the old NG+2R achievements.
  • Eternal Studies are being changed to serve as minor quality of life improvements and buffs.
  • Removed Investment completely; Elementary Research Upgrades have completely replaced it.
  • Quarks generate gluons that are used for Quantum Research Upgrades.

    • Balance

    Note that several of these changes are inspired by or taken from the Reality update and adapted to fit the needs of the modification.
    Quantum in this mod is going to last until 1e9e15 antimatter.

  • Early game progression changes (NG+2 Respecced is much different than the original NG++)
    • Temporary boost
      • Antimatter Dimension multipliers are now raised to ^1.1 after all other multipliers (exponent decreases based on how close you are to 1e4000 EP)
      • You get 16x more IP.
    • This is outside of the temporary boost, but there are several Reality update changes and my own changes that make Infinity and Eternity much easier to go through.
      • Numerous cost changes to Infinity Upgrades were made.
      • Autobuyers are much cheaper.
      • Eternity Upgrade 1 now always has at least 2x multiplier.
      • Achievement multipliers have improved (this has already been implemented), and a couple more new rewards.
      • "Eternities are the new infinity" now always gives at least 5x Eternities and a time boost (based on NG^^'s reward).
      • "We COULD afford 9" raises Replicanti multiplier to ^1.09 after time study effects.
      • "Faster than a potato^286078" now slightly decreases the replicanti interval based on tickspeed decrease.
      • "Unique snowflakes" now includes Antimatter Galaxies (galaxies/4) in the formulas of Time Study 102 and 103, and makes Replicanti Galaxies 0.05% more powerful.
      • "Now you're thinking with dilation!" raises all Dimension multipliers to ^1.01 and gives double TT and DT production while Dilated.
      • A few eternity milestones have been added to alleviate the grind.
    • As a general change, Eternity Challenges are going to be made easier to complete; you just have to reach the challenge completion tier's respective goal first. (post-Quantum)
  • Resets are now based on the best prestige currency instead of current.
  • Limited EP multiplier: 1e100,000 EP is now the hard limit for buying EP multipliers.
  • Limited Infinity Dimensions: You can now only buy 2,500,000 of each Infinity Dimension, to prevent reality from breaking. However, you may be able to extend the limit...
  • Limited Time Dimensions: Time Dimensions, except for the 8th Time Dimension, will no longer have multiplier increases after 500,000 purchases.
  • Tickspeed upgrades will also scale much faster after 1,000,000 tickspeed upgrades.
  • Heavily nerfed Meta Dimensions (^8 -> ^5) - the Dilation upgrade has been changed along with this (^5 -> ^6).
  • Meta-Antimatter Galaxies will be kept, but their cost scales after buying a few galaxies and their effect increase will slowly decrease.
  • The rebuyable 2x IP multiplier upgrade can no longer be bought after 1e100,000,000 IP.
